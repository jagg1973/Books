## B. Items left for your decision (not changed)

You asked for these as notes rather than edits, because they affect canon/meaning and only you should decide. My recommendation is given, but nothing here was altered.

### B1. Matthew 16:18 wording — Ch31 (now Ch31)
Michael's line at the gates of Hell: *"We ride to the gates of Hell, and we will not prevail against them. We will remind them that they have already fallen."*

- In Matthew 16:18 it is the **gates of hell that "shall not prevail"** against the Church — so a literal reading of Michael's line is inverted (he is the attacker, yet says *he* won't prevail).
- **However**, it may well be **intentional**: it fits the book's recurring theme that the victory is already won and the angels come not to conquer but to "remind them that they have already fallen."
- **Recommendation:** Likely intentional — leave as is. If you want the scriptural echo unambiguous, change to *"and they will not prevail against us."* Your call.

### B2. Name clash: "Azazel" vs "Azazyel"
- **Azazyel** — the King's Prosecutor, a Minister-class demon (introduced Ch3).
- **Azazel** — a twin-blade warrior champion (Ch34).
- Two different characters, near-identical names — a real risk of reader confusion.
- **Recommendation:** Rename one. "Azazel" is the well-known Watcher/fallen-angel name, so it may be best kept for the warrior; consider renaming the **prosecutor** (e.g. *Azaziah*, *Azrael*-adjacent alternatives, or another of your coinages). Tell me the replacement and I'll apply it consistently throughout and rebuild.

### B3. Name clash: "Lyra" (villain) vs "Lyra" (Nino's beloved)
- **Lyra** — leader of the Agapeion, a villain destroyed in Ch5.
- **Lyra** — the archivist who becomes Nino's companion and, in the finale (now Ch47–49), his wife.
- Sharing the heroine's name with an earlier destroyed villain is likely unintended.
- **Recommendation:** Rename one — most cleanly the **Ch5 villain**, since the beloved's name recurs through the ending and ties to the Spanish edition. Give me the new name and I'll apply it and rebuild both the English and (if you want) Spanish editions.
